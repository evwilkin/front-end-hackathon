About

Erin and I were married in August 2015, and pursuing our love of travel we planned an extended Honeymoon abroad in Europe where we had been separately but never together.

This site was built as both a way to share some of our memories with friends and family, and also as a chance to learn and use fullPage.js, Foundation and HTML video. It was completed in one day as a Hackathon project, though I look forward to using it as a project to continue to grow and learn from.
